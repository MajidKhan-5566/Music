COUNTRY MUSIC

INTRODUCTION

Acoustic Guitar, Electric Guitar, Banjo, Drums, Piano, Harmonica & Pedal Steel Guitar

Country music, originating in the rural Southern United States in the 1920s, is characterized by its storytelling lyrics and traditional instrumentation. Often featuring acoustic guitars, banjos, fiddles, and steel guitars, country music reflects themes of love, heartache, and rural life. It blends elements of folk, blues, and Western music, and has evolved into various subgenres, including honky-tonk, bluegrass, and contemporary country.
