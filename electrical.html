INTRODUCTION

Analog Synthesizer, Digital Synthesizer,MIDI Keyboard Controller, Digital Piano Modular Synthesizer & Drum Machine.

Electronic music is created using electronic devices and digital technologies, enabling a vast range of sound experimentation and manipulation. It encompasses various genres, from techno and house to ambient and IDM, characterized by synthetic sounds and innovative production techniques. This genre revolutionized music production, allowing artists to create entirely new auditory experiences.
