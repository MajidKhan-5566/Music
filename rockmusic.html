INTRODUCTION;

Electric Guitar, Bass Guitar, Drums, Acoustic Guitar, Keyboard, Piano, Harmonica & Trumpe.t

Rock music, a genre born in the 1950s, is known for its powerful rhythms, electric guitars, and strong beats. It emerged from a fusion of earlier genres like blues, country, and jazz, quickly becoming a dominant force in popular music. Characterized by its emphasis on electric guitar riffs and solos, rock music often features dynamic vocals and energetic performances. Over the decades, it has evolved into various subgenres, including classic rock, punk rock, and alternative rock.
