ALI ZAFAR

INTRODUCTION

Ali Zafar PP (born 18 May 1980) is a Pakistani singer-songwriter, actor, model, producer, screenwriter and painter. He started out on Pakistani television before becoming a popular musician, later also established a career in Bollywood and his success led many Pakistani actors to venture into Hindi films. He has received five Lux Style Awards and a Filmfare Award nomination. Ali Zafar, is an icon in the world of entertainment and can be rated as one of the most versatile and entertaining artists of modern times. His first album Huqa Pani in 2003 broke records selling 5 million copies in days and his latest hit “Jhoom” has the highest number of Instagram reels with 1.8 million reels made by fans lip syncing and dancing to the song. Zafar has since produced massive hit singles and Pakistan’s biggest non Eid holiday blockbuster “Teefa in Trouble”. Zafar was the first Pakistani actor to have been cast as lead in 7 Bollywood movies with many awards to his claim including “Best Debu” is stardust awards. Zafar has won countless awards and accolades including one of the highest civil awards from the Government of Pakistan “The Pride of Performance.”
