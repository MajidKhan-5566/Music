INTRODUCTION

Violin, Viola, Cello, Double Bass& Harp

Classical music is a genre that encompasses a broad range of styles from the medieval period to the present day, known for its intricate structures and formal compositions. It often features orchestras and chamber ensembles, with a focus on harmony, melody, and the exploration of various musical forms.it is characterized by its emphasis on precision, balance, and thematic development, showcasing the works of renowned composers like Bach, Mozart, and Beethoven. It remains influential, shaping the foundation of Western musical traditions and continuing to inspire contemporary composers and musicians.Its timeless appeal lies in its ability to convey deep emotion and complex ideas through meticulously crafted compositions.
