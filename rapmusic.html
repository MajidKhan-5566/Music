RAP MUSIC

INTRODUCTION;

Drum Machine, Sampler, Turntables, Synthesizer, Bass Guitar, Keyboard, Electronic Drum Kit, Acoustic Drum Kit & Piano.

Rap music, also known as hip-hop, is a genre characterized by rhythmic vocal style known as rapping, where artists deliver lyrics in a rhythmic and often rhyming manner. Emerging from the streets of the Bronx in the 1970s, rap music has evolved into a powerful cultural force with global reach. It often features strong beats, intricate wordplay, and commentary on social and political issues. The genre's influence extends beyond music into fashion, dance, and language, shaping contemporary pop culture. Rap music's dynamic and diverse nature continues to innovate, reflecting the experiences and voices of its artists.
